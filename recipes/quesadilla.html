Quesadilla

Description

This recipe details the ingredients required and outlines steps for making quesadillas. The filling will be added to a tortilla wrap along with cheese to make a quesadilla. The estimated times for this recipe are 10 minutes for preparation and 15 minutes for cooking (25 minutes total).

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Grated cheese
  • Sweetcorn
  • Salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, dried oregano, paprika, onion and cumin powder
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves
  • Onion
  • Minced beef
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • 65ml water

Steps

  1. Assemble quesadilla spice mix with 1 tsp each onion powder, dried oregano, salt, 2 tsp each cumin powder, paprika, and 1/4 tsp each black pepper, cayenne pepper (optional). Leave aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add onion and minced garlic, cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add beef and cook, breaking it up while it changes colour from pink to brown. Once browned, add diced bell pepper and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato paste, water and spice mix. Cook for 2 minutes. After, transfer to bowl and allow it to cool.
  5. Place tortilla on clean surface. Sprinkle one side with a bit of cheese, top with cooled quesadilla filling. Sprinkle with sweetcorn and top with cheese. Fold the empty half over.
  6. Preheat wok over medium low heat, if non-stick add 2 tsp oil.
  7. Place quesadilla in wok, press down lightly and cook until underside is golden brown and crispy.
  8. Carefully flip over to the other side. Press down lightly and cook again until crispy.
  9. Move quesadilla to chopping board and cut in half. Serve immediately on its own or with guacamole, salsa or sour cream.
